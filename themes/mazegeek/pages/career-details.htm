Job Description

MazeGeek's in-house Design team is responsible for the development and implementation of design solutions for different internal marketing collaterals; advertising and promotions; sales; support and training materials.

The team ensure to have a keen understanding of organization’s strategic goals, marketing goals, branding, a strong grasp of business needs, and how the company want to be perceived by its consumers. The team ensure all artwork produced meets the needs, requirements and brand values. The team includes Graphic Designers, Illustrators, Web Designers and Developer, UX Designer, Animators and Video production/post production expertise.

Responsibilities

  • Adverts and Flyers for both Print and Digital Media
  • Static and animated Ads for website, blogs and Social Media
  • Email newsletters for promotions, information and announcements
  • Website design, development and maintenance
  • Display booths, backdrops, stickers and different types of banners for events
  • Catalogs, Brochures and Postcards
  • Animation (GIF and Video)
  • Multimedia Production including filming, editing and post-production
  • Training Videos

What we are looking for:

  • Ability to utilise ideation skills, software knowledge, and innovation
  • Visual problem solver and good understanding of target audiences’ needs and goals
  • Bachelor’s degree required; preferably in CSE or related field
  • Strong portfolio which shows experience with a range of Graphics software and materials
  • Strong communications skills

Benefits

  • 2 days of weekend
  • 2 annual performanc Bonuses
  • Lunch & Snacks
